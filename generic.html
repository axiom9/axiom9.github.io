Why Data Science?

I was initially introduced to the concept of data science in May 2020. I was rather unfamiliar with this term and a quick google search conveyed that it had to do with one of my toughest challenges; programming.

After countless hours of pulling at my hair in frustration and coffee dates with my 'Python for dummies' guide at my local library, things finally clicked and the gears started turning!

I quickly came to love programming, as time passed I wanted to learn more and my love for artificial intelligence began to grow. I found it absolutely fascinating that one day, perhaps in the near future our lives can become very much intertwined with artificial intelligence. My goal is to work towards simplifying lives across the world by implementing artificial intelligence and using AI methods for analyzing big data to produce meaningful results that can directly impact the lives of others.

One thing I'd want you to know about me is that I absolutely love learning. I feel that there is so much to learn in this field and I want to soak it all up like a dry sponge. This may sound odd, but you know the feeling you get after working hard all day? The feeling in your head that your brain is 'fatigued' from a long day... I love that feeling. I try to push myself every day so that I can feel that at the end of my day because I know that's the number one sign that I pushed myself to work hard that day, soaked in as much knowledge I can, and be the best version of myself.
